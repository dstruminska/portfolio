04| STELLA ONE

2022-2023 PROJECT
VW CRAFTER CAMPERVAN CONVERSION

A self-planned, self-designed and self-built custom campervan conversion. Stella One is a year long project undertaken with my partner aimed to create a tiny living space in a VW Crafter van. It includes a bedroom space with a storage, a full shower with 100 l water tank, a full kitchen as well as living/working/dining and seating area for up to three people. An option to dismount the table, bench and the third seat provides with a possibility to obtain some extra space - either for goods transportation or and additional bed. An under-bed storage accessible form the inside and from the back provide with a sufficient space for some outdoor equipment.

INITIAL DESIGN| SKETCHES

In order to plan out the layout of the particular spaces in the van sketch plans and sections have been created. Rough
dimensions helped to guarantee everything will fit on the very limited area. Rough sketches of the kitchen units helped to
plan out the location of some of the services and sped up the actual build.

VISUALIZATION| RENDERS

In order to decide what colours, materials and textures should be used for the internal finish of
the space a series of renders has been created.

Additionally, this virtual model helped to evaluate the planned out layout in terms of access to
natural light, ability to move around the space and views to the outside.
