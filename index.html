· WORK PORTFOLIO ·

Throughout my university course my main focus and passion concentrated on architectural design through hand drawing - my manual skills let me to freely experiment with and discover the art of architectural design with no limitations at its initial phase. My attention to detail and good understanding of three-dimensional spaces let me later translate those concepts into actual projects with a help of particular softwares. The combination of creativity, logic and sense of aesthetics is what I find very alluring in the field and therefore am keen to further develop those skills and apply them during the RIBA part 1 apprenticeship or some other design-related profession.

A real life side-project - a campervan conversion - I have independently undertaken during my third year of university was a realistic test to my skills as a designer and a craftswoman. Building out a little home on the space of less than 8 square meters turned out to be a great opportunity to apply my knowledge on construction and design as well as my manual skills.
